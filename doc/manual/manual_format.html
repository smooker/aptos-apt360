Part Programming Manual Format

This manual is the only document required to use the N/C 360 APT language. It has been assembled such that it can be used either as a cross-reference manual for experienced part programmer or as a learning guide for those unfamiliar with the application.

By necessity, then, the manual begins with the essentials of the language and progresses from the simple concepts to the more complex notions. Some items are self-explanatory and therefore are discussed only once in the entire manual. Other topics are introduced early in the manual and are then expanded in later sections. This permits the reader to grasp the rudiments of a feature without getting lost in the intricacies until knowledge has been gained to facilitate a more complete understanding.

Chapter 14 includes more complete discussions of many concepts introduced earlier. Ways to use the language in a more sophisticated manner, coupled with appropriate warnings as to possible problems areas, are given.
