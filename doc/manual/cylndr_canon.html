16.5. CYLNDR

Canonical form is (X, Y, Z, A, B, C, R)

where (X, Y, Z) is a point on the axis of the cylinder, (A, B, C) are the direction components of a unit vector parallel to the axis, and (R) is the radius of the cylinder.
