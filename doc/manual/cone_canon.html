16.8. CONE

Canonical form is (X, Y, Z, A, B, C, R, COSTH)

where (X, Y, Z) are the coordinates of the vertex, (A, B, C) are the direction components of a unit vector parallel to the axis, and COSTH is the cosine of the vertex half-angle.
