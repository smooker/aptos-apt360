4.4. Implication of the ZSURF Statement

The ZSURF statement (see Section 3.2) can be used to supply the z coordinate when the alternate form for the FROM or GOTO statement is used (that is, FROM/ x, y or GOTO/ x, y). The z coordinate is computed from the plane defined by the ZSURF statement and is zero if no ZSURF statement is specified.
